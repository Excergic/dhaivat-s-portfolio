Predicts whether an order will be delivered late **at the moment it is placed**, giving businesses a chance to act before it's too late.

Built on a real-world supply chain dataset, the model surfaces delivery risk early — so operations teams can reroute, prioritize, or notify customers proactively instead of reacting after the fact.
